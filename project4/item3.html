The Origin Movie Poster

The movie is called “The Origin” in which a group of college students have been assigned to observe and study an anomaly in which spiders have grown to abnormal sizes. As the group searches for the true origin of the spiders, they encounter a menacing giant spider that that has no intent to let them leave alive. This movie poster combines two images, one of the spider web and the other of the spider itself. This project involved the heavy use of layer mask and an attention to details. Adobe Photoshop was used to design this movie poster.
