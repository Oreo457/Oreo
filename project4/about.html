ABOUT ME

Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Kevin Arevalo. I am a current Digital Media & Web Technology student at the Univeristy of Maryland Global Campus. I am an individual who is genuinely curious in learning and trying new things. Over the course of my studies, I have experimented with different styles, techniques, and mediums with respect to my designs. I particularly enjoy creating stories for my works. Stories that give the reader some context on the ideas and emotions behind the works. Some of my hobbies are sketching and drawing with charcoal, going out for nature walks, collecting Lego sets, and listening to music. I hope that you enjoy my portfolio.
