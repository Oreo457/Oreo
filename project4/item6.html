Blue Dress Girl Digital Painting

This digital painting was done as a favor for a friend and is based on actual photo of her. Although I have never worked with digital painting, I have worked with traditional painting tools.This digital painting was made in Procreate for iPad. The process involved sketches as well as selecting particular textured brushes and contained around 40 layers. I put effort into every detail and proportion to make sure I captured a realistic look with the painting.
