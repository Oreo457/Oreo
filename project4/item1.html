Glacier National Park Poster

This poster beautifully and simplistically depicts the beautiful scenary of Saint Mary Lake at Glacier National Park. The idea of this poster origianlly came from a sketch to a digital painting to a vectorial illustration. It features simplistic shapes but also contains small details such as the gradient in the sky and clouds as well as the reflections on the lake. This poster is intended for those who love flat design and nature. Adobe Illustrator was used to create every single shape that makes up the moutains to the trees.
