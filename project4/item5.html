The Holy Ruckus Logo

The Holy Ruckus is an online Catholic social media platform where I was tasked to design a new logo. The new Holy Ruckus (top) Logo keeps the same color palette of the old logo but adds a touch of a minimalistic flat design aesthetic. The logo makes use of a gradient on the word "Holy" and incorporates a halo into the R. Adobe Illustrator was used to design this logo. Monochrome variations in yellow and black were designed to be used as alternatives to the main logo.
